The Best Rooftops in Cali: Experience the Magic of the City from Above

Cali is a vibrant city, full of energy, culture, and a nightlife that never stops. But if you want to experience the salsa capital from a different perspective, rooftops are the best option. There’s nothing like enjoying a cocktail, great music, and a spectacular panoramic view of the Sultana of the Valley.

In this blog, we present to you the 5 best rooftops in Cali, carefully selected to offer you a unique experience from above.

1. Don Juan de la Torre Rooftop

Location: Torre de Cali, 42nd Floor

The Best: The highest view in the Colombian southwest

Experience: A sophisticated atmosphere with a blend of signature cocktails, live music, and gourmet cuisine.

If you're looking for the best view of the city, Don Juan de la Torre Rooftop is the ideal spot. Located in the iconic Torre de Cali, this venue offers an elegant atmosphere with exclusive events and unforgettable sunsets. Perfect for a romantic date or a night out with friends enjoying Cali's breeze.

Don Juan de la Torre Rooftop Instagram

2. Primitivo Rooftop

Location: Plaza del Lili Hotel, Cali

The Best: A fusion of gastronomy and mixology in a modern setting

Experience: Brunch-style breakfasts, dinners, and innovative cocktails.

This rooftop blends the ancestral with the modern in every detail. Its daily brunch is the perfect way to start the day with a spectacular view and a top-tier culinary experience. At night, the ambiance transforms with warm lighting and live music, ideal for relaxing.

Official Website of Primitivo Rooftop

3. Sunset Rooftop Terraza Cali

Location: Calle 16 #84A-11, Barrio Ingenio

The Best: Magical sunsets and a cozy atmosphere

Experience: A perfect spot to enjoy the sunset with great drinks and company.

This rooftop lives up to its name, offering unparalleled sunsets over the city. With relaxed decor and a warm ambiance, Sunset Rooftop is ideal for sharing a cocktail after work or enjoying a quiet evening with live music.

Sunset Rooftop Instagram

4. Rooftop 8.17

Location: Cali, Valle del Cauca

The Best: A youthful atmosphere and 2-for-1 promotions

Experience: Perfect for groups of friends and casual outings with delicious food.

If you're looking for a rooftop with a relaxed vibe and great promotions, Rooftop 8.17 is the perfect choice. It is known for its 2-for-1 wings and varied cocktail menu. An ideal spot to enjoy great food and share moments with friends while taking in the city from above.

Rooftop 8.17 Instagram

5. Eva Rooftop

Location: Cra. 2 Oeste #1-07, El Peñón

The Best: Elegance and premium gastronomy

Experience: A sophisticated rooftop with excellent cuisine and an exclusive atmosphere.

For those who love fine dining and an exclusive ambiance, Eva Rooftop is the perfect choice. Its signature cuisine and premium cocktails make it an ideal destination for a special dinner or an elegant evening with friends.

Eva Rooftop Instagram

Which Will You Visit First?

Each of these rooftops offers a unique experience in Cali. Whether you prefer a relaxed atmosphere with dreamy views or an elegant space with top-notch cuisine, the city has the perfect rooftop for you.
